    Children love and parents trust

    Yateland is a technological and educational company, crafting children’s apps since 2011. Over the last decade, its products have accumulated 150 million downloads and are loved by children and parents in over 200 countries and regions.

    Many of Yateland’s apps focus on dinosaur characters, including the well-known Tyrannosaurus, Triceratops, and Velociraptor. Yateland also incorporates lesser-known dinosaurs, such as Pachycephalosaurus and Parasaurolophus. Most of them can be found and played in Dinosaur Park.

    While working hard to develop educational apps, Yateland’s game products have also been upgraded. Dinosaur Rockets pulls kids into a world of imaginative play. From launching rockets and space shuttles to entering orbits, the in-game designs follow real-world situations while adapting them for the curious minds of children. That allows kids’ creativity and imagination to be cultivated naturally.
